Our Mission

StudyBuddy democratizes access to high-quality educational tools by leveraging artificial intelligence. How StudyBuddy Works

1. Upload Materials

Upload PDFs, images, or documents. Our OCR technology extracts text automatically.

2. Edit & Organize

Use our rich text editor to refine content, add notes, and organize by subjects and topics.

3. Generate Questions

AI creates personalized practice questions based on your study materials and learning style.

4. Practice & Learn

Take practice sessions, track progress, and receive detailed feedback on your performance.
